Install the SkillsPath Extension

The extension reads your Canvas courses and IBM SkillsBuild history so the AI advisor can build a personalized roadmap. Takes about 30 seconds.

1
Download skillspath-extension.zip below and unzip it anywhere.
2
Open chrome://extensions in Chrome and enable Developer mode (toggle, top-right corner).
3
Click Load unpacked → select the unzipped extension folder. Then open your school's Canvas and IBM SkillsBuild to sync your data.
